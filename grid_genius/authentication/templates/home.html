About Design

Welcome to the Electricity Budget Predictor! Our goal is to help you estimate your electricity bill based on your appliance usage. Simply input the number of appliances, wattage, and usage duration, and our tool will calculate your estimated electricity consumption and cost. By tracking your usage, you can stay informed and make energy-efficient decisions for your home!
